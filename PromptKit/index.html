PromptKit Privacy Policy

Last updated: December 2025

PromptKit respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how your information is handled when you use the PromptKit mobile application.

Information We Collect

PromptKit does not require you to create an account and does not collect personally identifiable information such as your name, email address, phone number, or location.

Any prompts you bookmark or save are stored locally on your device and are not transmitted to our servers.

Analytics

PromptKit may use anonymous analytics to understand general app usage such as screen views, button interactions, and session duration. This data is used solely to improve app performance and user experience.

Analytics data does not identify you personally and is never sold or shared with third parties.

Ads

PromptKit does not display ads in its current version.

Data Sharing

PromptKit does not sell, trade, or share your personal data with third parties.

Permissions

PromptKit does not request or require any device permissions to function. The app does not access your contacts, messages, camera, microphone, location, storage, or any other sensitive device features.

Children’s Privacy

PromptKit is not directed toward children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated as the app evolves. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated revision date.
